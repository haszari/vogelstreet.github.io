The Vogel Street Party takes place on Saturday, the 18th of October from 3 pm to 10pm in Vogel Street, just south of the Dunedin CBD.

Vogel Street will be splashed with colour, arts and lights for its family friendly street party on Saturday 18 October. From 3 - 10pm, music will flow from a stage while the street will evolve into an active art space, divided into clusters of activities, performers, exhibitions and vendors.
